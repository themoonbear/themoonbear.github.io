<!-- more -->

## Features

Reward:
* First login reward apples and bananas
* Ads video reward apples and bananas
* Win in match mode rewarded apples

Play tutorial mode:
* No network required

Play friend mode:
* Invite friends play together required a banana

Play match mode:
* Play with other players required 200 apples
